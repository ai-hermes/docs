This guide will help you make the most of Rethink AI and extract more value from your analysis reports.

## Understanding Analysis Reports

### Personality Profile

The personality profile is based on the Big Five personality model and evaluates conversation partners across five dimensions:

- **Extraversion**  
  Reflects social tendencies. High scores indicate outgoing and talkative behavior, while low scores indicate introversion and quietness.
- **Openness**  
  Reflects acceptance of new ideas. High scores indicate curiosity and creativity, while low scores indicate practicality and tradition.
- **Conscientiousness**  
  Reflects work attitude. High scores indicate organization and reliability, while low scores indicate spontaneity and flexibility.
- **Agreeableness**  
  Reflects interpersonal attitude. High scores indicate friendliness and cooperation, while low scores indicate competition and independence.
- **Emotional Stability**  
  Reflects emotional control. High scores indicate calmness and stability, while low scores indicate sensitivity and emotional volatility.

### Emotion Curve

The emotion curve shows how emotions change throughout the conversation:

- **Positive emotions**: happiness, excitement, satisfaction, and similar signals
- **Neutral emotions**: calm tone and factual description
- **Negative emotions**: dissatisfaction, worry, frustration, and similar signals

By observing the emotion curve, you can:

- Identify key moments of emotional fluctuation
- Understand which topics trigger strong emotional responses
- Assess the overall emotional tone of the conversation

### Intent Analysis

Intent analysis classifies needs expressed in a conversation into three types:

- **Explicit needs**: directly stated requests and demands
- **Implicit needs**: needs expressed indirectly through hints
- **Latent needs**: deeper needs inferred from context

## Using Analysis Results

### Improving Communication

Adjust your communication style based on personality results:

- Be more direct and enthusiastic with highly extraverted people
- Provide detailed information and clear plans for highly conscientious people
- Focus on emotional connection and relationship maintenance for highly agreeable people

### Timing Matters

Use the emotion curve to choose better timing:

- Make important requests when the other person's mood is positive
- Avoid sensitive discussions when they are emotionally low
- Pay attention to turning points and understand their triggers

### Meeting Needs

Respond more effectively based on intent analysis:

- Prioritize explicitly stated needs
- Proactively address implied expectations
- Anticipate potential concerns ahead of time

## Advanced Features

### Historical Comparison

Pro users can compare multiple analyses of the same contact and track long-term changes in personality signals and emotional patterns.

### Team Collaboration

The enterprise plan supports shared analysis results for teams such as sales and customer support.

### API Integration

The enterprise plan offers an API so you can integrate Rethink AI capabilities into your own systems.

## Best Practices

1. **Choose representative conversations**  
   Include multiple topics and situations for more reliable results.
2. **Update analyses regularly**  
   People change over time, so periodic re-analysis is useful.
3. **Combine results with real-world context**  
   AI analysis is a support tool, not a replacement for judgment.
4. **Respect privacy**  
   Get consent before analyzing conversations and do not misuse the results.
